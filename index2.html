About Me

I'm an enthusiastic Electronics and Computer Engineering student at the Malawi University of Business and Applied Sciences (MUBAS), with a passion for AI, data analysis, and design tools like Figma and MATLAB l. I embrace both the technical and creative aspects of engineering which means diving into embedded systems and frontend design, while also exploring user interfaces and analytic workflows. My journey has been shaped by a love for problem-solving and a desire to create impactful solutions. Collaborating across teams is something I value deeply, I’ve worked together with peers to deliver standout projects like our award-winning MUBAS flag design. That teamwork, creativity, and shared vision is what drives my motivation .When I need a mental reset, I turn to design especially web layout experiments in Figma. It’s refreshing and inspires new ideas, balancing the logic of coding with the artistry of user experience
